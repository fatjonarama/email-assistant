Security & Privacy

Your data is safe.
By design.

🔒

MailMind can only read and label your emails. It cannot send, delete, forward, or share any email on your behalf.

No Email Content Stored
Email body content is never saved. Only the subject, sender, and classification label are logged in Notion.
↩️

Remove MailMind's access instantly from Google Account → Security → Third-party apps. No questions asked.

Encrypted Connection
